Privacy Policy

S Advanced Attendance System — Last updated:

S Advanced Attendance System ("we," "our," or "the App") provides workplace attendance management for organizations and their employees. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, who can access it, and the choices available to you.

By creating an account or otherwise using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who This Policy Covers

The App serves two types of accounts:

  • Company Admins — organizations that register to manage staff attendance.
  • Staff Members — employees added by an Admin to record attendance.

Each company's data is kept separate and private. Admins can only access their own organization's staff and records, and staff can only access their own information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Company Admins: company name, email address, phone number, account password, a security PIN, and an optional company logo.
  • Staff Members: name, email address, phone number, account password, and a system-generated Staff ID.

Passwords and security PINs are never stored in plain text and are protected using industry-standard security measures.

Attendance Information

We record the date, time, and type (check-in or check-out) of each attendance entry, and, where a company uses shift-based scheduling, which shift the entry belongs to.

Location Information

Where an Admin enables location-based verification ("geofencing") and you grant permission, we collect your device's location at the moment you check in or out, to confirm attendance occurred at the registered work location. Location is never collected in the background or outside of a check-in/check-out action, and you may withdraw location permission at any time through your device or browser settings. If location permission is withheld and geofencing is required by your Admin, check-in or check-out may not be available.

Technical and Device Information

We automatically collect limited technical information — such as your IP address and device recognition information — to help secure accounts, detect suspicious activity, and, for Company Admins, recognize devices that have previously been approved to access the account. We do not use this information for advertising, cross-app tracking, or profiling, and we do not use cookies for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Record and report attendance accurately
  • Verify identity during sign-in, check-in, and check-out
  • Send account-related communications, such as password reset codes and security notifications
  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Generate attendance reports and analytics for the relevant company Admin

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Who Can Access Your Information

  • Company Admins can view attendance records, contact details, and location data (where enabled) for staff within their own company only.
  • Staff Members can view only their own attendance history and profile information.
  • Authorized personnel supporting the App may access information only as necessary to operate, maintain, or secure the service.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on trusted third-party service providers to help operate the App, including providers of cloud infrastructure and hosting, secure data storage, and email and messaging delivery (for example, to send password reset codes and account notifications). These providers process information only as needed to deliver their services to us and are not permitted to use it for their own independent purposes.

Verify before publishing: confirm that each provider has an appropriate data processing agreement in place, and check whether your jurisdiction requires you to name specific providers or disclose the country/region where data is stored.

6. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encrypted connections, access-controlled data storage, and additional verification steps when sign-in is attempted from a new or unrecognized device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected Admins as soon as reasonably possible.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active and as needed to provide the App. If a staff member is removed by their Admin, their historical attendance records may be retained for legitimate business record-keeping purposes, while login credentials and device-recognition data associated with that staff member are deleted. If a company account is deleted, all associated data is permanently removed.

Verify before publishing: confirm and, if helpful, state a specific deletion timeframe (e.g., "within 30 days") once this is finalized on the backend.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw location permission at any time

Staff members should generally direct requests to their company Admin. Admins may contact us directly using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for workplace use by employed adults and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or operational reasons. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
