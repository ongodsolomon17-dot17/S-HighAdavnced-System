Privacy Policy

S Advanced Attendance System — Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how S Advanced Attendance System ("we," "our," "the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our attendance management service.

By creating an account or using this App as a staff member, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who This Applies To

This App is used by two types of accounts:

  • Company Admins — businesses who register to track staff attendance
  • Staff Members — employees added by an Admin to clock in and out

Each company's data is private to that company. Admins cannot see other companies' staff, and staff cannot see other companies' data.

2. Information We Collect

From Admins: Company name, email address, phone number, password and security PIN (stored encrypted), profile picture / company logo (optional), and device information used to verify trusted devices (browser type, screen size, timezone).

From Staff Members: Name, email address, phone number, password (stored encrypted), and a unique Staff ID generated by the system.

Attendance & Location Data: Timestamp of every check-in/check-out, GPS coordinates at the moment of check-in or check-out (only if you grant location permission), and which shift (day or night) the record belongs to.

We collect location data solely to confirm attendance happened at the registered work location (geofencing), if the Admin has enabled this feature. Location is not tracked continuously, only at the moment of a check-in/check-out action.

Automatically Collected: IP address (basic security and rate-limiting) and device fingerprint (recognizing trusted devices only, not cross-app tracking).

We do not collect or use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Record and report attendance accurately
  • Verify your identity when you check in or out
  • Send password reset codes (via email, SMS, or WhatsApp)
  • Send important account or device-approval notifications
  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Generate attendance reports and analytics for your company's Admin

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Who Can See Your Information

  • Company Admins can see attendance records, contact details, and location data (if enabled) of staff within their own company only.
  • Staff Members can see their own attendance history and profile information only.
  • We (the developers) can access data only as necessary to maintain, debug, or secure the service.

6. Data Security

  • Passwords and PINs are hashed before storage — never stored as plain text
  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • Failed login attempts are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks
  • New or unrecognized devices require Admin approval before staff can access the account
  • Database access is restricted and not publicly accessible

No system can guarantee 100% security. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify affected Admins as soon as reasonably possible.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active. If a staff member is removed by an Admin, their attendance records may be retained for record-keeping purposes, but login credentials and device trust records are deleted. If a company account is deleted, all associated data is permanently deleted.

8. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw location permission at any time through your browser settings

9. Location Data — Specifically

  • Location is only requested at the moment you tap Check In or Check Out
  • We do not track your location in the background or at any other time
  • You can deny location permission, but your Admin's geofencing setting may prevent check-in without it
  • Location data is visible only to your company's Admin, never to other companies or the public

10. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for workplace use by employed adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 16.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
